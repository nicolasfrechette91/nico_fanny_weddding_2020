Airport Information

The best way to reach the beautiful, cosmopolitan and international city of Aguascalientes is arriving directly to the International airport (AGU - Jesus-Teran Peredo International Airport).

The Internation airport of Aguascalientes recieves flights from Dallas, Houston and Chicago. Therefore, we would strongly recommend to take those to arrive.
